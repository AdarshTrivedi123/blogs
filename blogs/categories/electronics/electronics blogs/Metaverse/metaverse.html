METAVERSE

INTRODUCTION:


Imagine you can create your perfect model of anything that exists in the real world, in a virtual world where you have control of all aspects. You can do whatever you want, and you probably have unlimited possibilities. Such scenes were frequently portrayed in films of science fiction movies or series.


Metaverse is therefore a merging of virtual, augmented, and physical reality, capable of changing the world. As it allows people to go to places they would never be able to go and explore things they would never be able to see. By accessing 3D virtual spaces, solutions, and user-created environments. Here people can work, trade, play, and create. It uses advanced virtualization and technology (AR, VR, Haptic sensors, etc.) to immerse the user fully in the virtual world.


TECHNOLOGY BEHIND METAVERSE


Blockchain and Cryptocurrency


Cryptocurrencies increase users' ability to exchange value while working and having fun in the 3D digital world. Using blockchain technology, it is possible to establish and protect the ownership of virtual spaces. So Crypto may one day encourage people to work in the metaverse. More and more companies are moving their offices online, increasing the number of metaverse-related activities.


Augmented reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR)


AR works with three basic techniques: real-time interaction, 3-D visualization, and coupling of real and virtual environments. AR can be used on any camera-enabled device, and that is why it is preferred over VR apps. Their adoption rate is high. Consumers do not need extra clothing to get AR help


With VR, users can experience the same physical sensations. Here additional equipment such as a head-mounted display or multimodal projection screen is required. The combination of AR and VR is better known as extended reality (XR).


Artificial intelligence (AI)


With the help of AI engines, we can produce avatars that look realistic and accurate by analyzing 2D images and 3D scanners. In addition, to develop the digital people we create, AI can also be used to create facial expressions, hair extensions, costumes, and other features that will be used in VR games. Through AI, NPCs (non-player characters) people can interact in 3D environments and have conversations with users or perform other tasks.


3D Reconstruction


One of the challenges of the metaverse is to create a digital environment that seems as close to our real world as possible. With the help of 3D reconstruction, it can create realistic and natural-looking environments. With specialized 3D cameras, we can take our world online by providing accurate 3D photo models of buildings, scenes, and objects. 3D location data and 4K HD images and then transmitted to computers to process and produce the virtual image in the metaverse for users to experience. These virtual representations of physical objects can also be called digital twins.


Internet of Things (IoT)


IoT is a system that captures everything in our physical world and connects it to the internet via sensors and devices. These gadgets will have a unique identifier and the ability to send and receive information automatically after being connected to the internet. Metaverse's IoT applications are designed to collect and distribute data from the physical world. By doing so, the digital presentations will be more accurate.


METAVERSE PROJECTS


As Metaverse is a leading tech brand, the entire industry jumps to its horizon, leading to significant growth for Metaverse projects. Here are top Metaverse projects –


Gaming Metaverse-Decentraland


Decentraland is a game-based decentralized Metaverse project designed to help the real world and global users of the world to build, test, and trade non-fungible NFT tokens. It allows users to purchase land within the world, manage live events, play games, and perform additional fun activities, which are also possible in the real world.


Real estate Metaverse-Upland


Upland is a Metaverse project powered by a blockchain designed to support the purchase, sale, and virtual real estate from real-world addresses. The properties on this platform are represented by NFTs that users can own to become “digital landowners” and trade these NFTs for profit in UPX coins.


Multipurpose Metaverse — Enjin


Enjin is a Metaverse project built and run on the Ethereum blockchain that supports the construction of markets in its area. The platform does not place a bar on project types, allowing developers to build market space from gaming to retail, marketing, and e-commerce. Enjin can be described in a few simple steps —acquisition, mining, gaming, trading, and melting.


LATEST DEVELOPMENT OF METAVERSE


  • As Facebook changed its name to Meta in October 2021, metaverse became the new favorite name. The idea is to improve the content of metaverse, software, and AR and VR headsets, as CEO Mark Zuckerberg believes it will spread across smartphones in the future.
  • 3D virtual spaces that allow colleagues to join meetings, and participation are increasing. The Microsoft Mesh launched in November 2021 is an example. It integrates immersive spaces so that users can meet and interact using their avatars, making remote group meetings attractive and enjoyable.
  • Nvidia Omniverse is an open platform designed to connect 3D spaces in a shared space to facilitate virtual interaction between engineers, designers, and creators. It is currently used in various industries. For example, BMW Group uses Omniverse to reduce production time and improve product quality through smart production.

WHY IS METAVERSE IMPORTANT


Metaverse can enhance scientific research


Metaverse can surpass scientific research in a few years, especially in healthcare, aerospace, manufacturing, and a host of other fields.


For example, pharmaceutical field professionals, can simulate the molecular structure of a vaccine in 3D, and then meet with project participants in a VR environment. Metaverse can quickly track authorization, improve error detection, and increase success rates.


It could be a global digital space


Metaverse could be a digital space where we all “live,” as is the case with social media today. It will determine the friendships, relationships, and flexibility of people, and can enable people from all over the world to meet face-to-face without having to travel.


CHALLENGES IN METAVERSE


Like all emerging technologies, Metaverse also faces potential challenges, and with this in mind, let’s look at those limitations.

  • Contrary to what we experience in the real world, it is challenging to identify people in the Metaverse. One has to reassure users by using an avatar voice, facial expressions, and features.
  • Scammers or bots who try to damage someone else's image can act as an image of other to achieve the ultimate goal.
  • Another important challenge is privacy concerns. Metaverse dependence on VR and AR devices can leak personal data because they are equipped with different camera capabilities and identifiers.
These challenges do not apply to the entire Metaverse project. With the full knowledge of Metaverse development and a professional team, one can easily design and present a custom-made real world, escaping those potential challenges.
